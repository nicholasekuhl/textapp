Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Veloxo ("Veloxo," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our SMS CRM platform for licensed insurance agents (the "Service"), accessible at veloxo.io and app.veloxo.io.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. Who We Are

Veloxo is operated by Kuhl Technologies LLC, a company registered in the United States. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@veloxo.io.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Your name, email address, agency name, phone number, business address, and timezone when you create an account or complete onboarding.
  • Lead information: Contact details and notes for the insurance leads you import or receive through our API, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, dates of birth, ZIP codes, states, household information, and any notes you record.
  • Message content: The full content of SMS messages sent and received through the platform, including messages authored by you, by our AI assistant on your behalf, and by the leads you communicate with.
  • Appointment and calendar data: Appointment details you create within Veloxo, including date, time, duration, title, and notes.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed by our payment provider. Veloxo does not store full credit card numbers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and timestamps of activity.
  • Device and log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and diagnostic data for debugging and security.
  • Cookies: Authentication and preference cookies. Veloxo does not use third-party advertising cookies.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party services such as Google Calendar, we receive information from those services as described in Section 4 below.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Send SMS messages to leads on your behalf through Twilio
  • Generate AI-assisted responses using Anthropic's Claude API, sending only the minimum context required (your lead's current conversation and relevant profile data) and never retaining conversations beyond the API call itself on Anthropic's side
  • Authenticate and authorize your access to the Service
  • Send you service notifications, product updates, and billing communications
  • Comply with legal obligations including TCPA compliance, state SMS regulations, and carrier requirements
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues
  • Deliver customer support

4. Google User Data

Google API Services User Data Policy — Limited Use Disclosure: Veloxo's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4.1 What We Access

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, Veloxo requests the following OAuth scopes:

  • calendar.events — to create, update, and delete calendar events corresponding to appointments you schedule in Veloxo.
  • calendar.readonly — to read your existing calendar events so they can be displayed alongside your Veloxo appointments within the Veloxo calendar view, helping you avoid scheduling conflicts.
  • userinfo.email — used once, at connection time, to identify which Google account you connected. We display this email in your settings so you can verify the correct account.

4.2 How We Use Google Data

  • Calendar events you read or write are used solely to provide the calendar synchronization feature.
  • External (non-Veloxo) calendar events are cached for display purposes only; we store the event title, start/end times, and an "all day" flag.
  • We do NOT use your Google data to train AI or machine learning models.
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your Google data.
  • We do NOT share your Google data with any third party except subprocessors strictly necessary to provide the Service (listed in Section 7).
  • We do NOT read, process, or display Google data beyond the calendar synchronization feature.

4.3 Tokens and Storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM before being stored in our database. Tokens are never exposed in frontend code or in API responses. Our server accesses Google APIs using these tokens only in response to your actions (creating an appointment, for example) or on scheduled intervals to keep your calendar view current.

4.4 Revocation and Deletion

You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from your Veloxo settings page. When you disconnect:

  • We revoke our access tokens with Google immediately.
  • We delete all cached external calendar events from our database.
  • We delete your stored OAuth tokens.
  • Appointments created in Veloxo that were synced to Google remain in your Google Calendar (we do not delete your data on Google's side when you disconnect).

You can also revoke Veloxo's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

5. Twilio and SMS Data

Veloxo uses Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Message content, phone numbers, and delivery metadata are transmitted to Twilio for the purpose of sending and receiving messages. Twilio's privacy practices are governed by Twilio's Privacy Policy. Veloxo stores the full content of messages in our database for the purpose of conversation history, AI context, and compliance recordkeeping.

6. AI and Automated Responses

Veloxo uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate AI-assisted replies to leads. When generating a reply, Veloxo sends the following to Anthropic:

  • The recent conversation history between you and the lead
  • Relevant lead profile fields (first name, state, age, household size, etc.) to inform context
  • Your agent profile information (name, agency, business address)

Anthropic processes these requests but, per Anthropic's policy, does not use API inputs to train their models and does not retain request data beyond what is required to return the response. For details, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

7. Subprocessors

We share data with the following subprocessors strictly as necessary to provide the Service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Railway — server hosting
  • Twilio — SMS delivery
  • Anthropic — AI response generation
  • Google — calendar integration (only if you enable it)
  • Real Phone Validation — DNC and phone number validation

8. Data Retention

  • Account data and lead data: retained while your account is active, and for 90 days after account deletion for backup and legal compliance, then permanently deleted.
  • Messages: the most recent 90 days of active messages are kept in the primary database; older messages are archived but remain retrievable for compliance purposes. Archived messages may be permanently deleted 2 years after the last activity on the associated lead.
  • Google OAuth tokens: retained while your Google integration is active. Deleted upon disconnection.
  • External calendar event cache: events are deleted when they are older than 30 days or when you disconnect Google.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to export your data in a portable format
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@veloxo.io. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security

Veloxo implements industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS encryption in transit for all connections
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data including OAuth tokens (AES-256-GCM)
  • Row-level security policies in our database for tenant isolation
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures
  • Limited employee access to production systems

No system is perfectly secure. If we learn of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you consistent with applicable law.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. International Users

Veloxo is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to your account email address at least 30 days before taking effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
