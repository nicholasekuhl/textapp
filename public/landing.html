What Veloxo does
Everything you need to work a book of leads
Lead Management
Import thousands of leads via CSV or lead vendor API. Organize them into buckets, folders, and custom pipelines. Track every touchpoint automatically.
Campaigns & Drips
Multi-step outreach sequences with spintext variation, timezone-aware sending, and disposition-based follow-up drips. Customize everything or start from a template.
Conversation Inbox
Every thread in one place. Star important leads, flag threads for review, track unread counts, and reply from your desk or your phone. You stay in control of every message that goes out.
Pipeline & Dispositions
Customizable dispositions trigger automated actions — follow-up drips, pipeline changes, notifications. Every lead moves through the stages you define, tracked in real time.
Appointments & Calendar
Built-in appointment scheduler with Google Calendar integration. Veloxo appointments push to your Google Calendar. Your personal events show up on Veloxo's calendar view so you never double-book.
Lead Vendor Integration
Plug in your lead sources via API or CSV. Map your vendor's fields to Veloxo's lead format once, and new leads flow in automatically — ready to be enrolled in your campaigns.
Compliance Built In
State-specific quiet hours, TCPA daily send caps, A2P 10DLC support, automatic opt-out handling, and DNC list checks. Every safeguard runs in the background so you don't have to think about it.
AI Assistance (Optional)
Turn on AI replies for specific leads when you can't get to them in time. The AI follows rules you set, hands off to you the moment things get serious, and never sends a message you haven't approved the playbook for.
Automation & Workflow
Scheduled messages, auto-follow-ups, ghost detection, pipeline transitions, and notification routing — all configurable per campaign and per disposition. Automation with manual override.
